About Me

My primary interest is understanding how humans interact with robots with a specific focus on powered exoskeletons. My expertise spans from designing mechatronic hardware to developing a high-level machine learning model. I focus on developing a smart robotic platform to enhance human mobility which encompasses both robust mechanical design and a novel intellectual controller. My long term research goal is to translate this technology to different clinical populations to help patients with gait deficiencies such as stroke, SCI, and muscular dystrophy.
